# Dashboard Documentation

## Dashboard Purpose

The dashboard is designed to help business users analyze customer churn and Customer Lifetime Value.

## Visualizations

- Total Customers KPI
- Churn Distribution
- Contract Type Analysis
- Monthly Charges
- Average Tenure
- Senior Citizen Distribution

## Filters

- Contract
- Gender
- Payment Method
- Internet Service
- Senior Citizen

## Benefits

- Monitor churn trends
- Understand customer behaviour
- Support business decision making
- Identify high-value customers
